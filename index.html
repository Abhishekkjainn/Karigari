About Us

We Here at Karigari Designs work closely with all the Local Artisans and provide them an opportunity for earning their Livelihood and fill out their bags. All the Artisans are verified. Our products are all Handmade and they are made with All natural materials that are Ecofriendly and Bio-Degradable and at the same time Very Reliable.We here at Karigari believe that Money should not be the only motive for us to work on what we are doing. So we provide Free Education to the artisans who are Illiterate and also their Families and Kids.All Our products are thoroughly tested and Safe to use.
